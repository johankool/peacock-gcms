Discover Peacock

With Peacock, making sense of your gas-chromatography/mass-spectrometry analyses suddenly has become much easier. It helps you by recognizing the peaks in your chromatograms. Next, the library search helps you identify the compounds that those peaks represents. Peacock even goes a step further, and helps you to make sense out of the data you obtained by creating handy summaries and calculating relevant ratios on the fly.
