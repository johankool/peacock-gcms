Working with chromatograms

When you are working with your chromatograms you can use several convenient key strokes and mouse events to home in on your data. The table below summarize the available shortcuts.

KeyDescription
aZoom view to fit all data
bToggle display baseline
fToggle display frame
gToggle display grid
lToggle display legend
mToggle display major tickmarks
nToggle display minor tickmarks
pToggle display peaks
xToggle display axes
↓Select next peak
↑Select previous peak
→Select next scan
←Select previous scan
⌥↑Pan upwards
⌥↓Pan downwards
⌥←Pan to the left
⌥→Pan to the right
⌦ or ⌫Delete selected peaks or baseline points
In Comparison Window
⌃↑Move chromatogram upwards
⌃↓Move chromatogram downwards
ModifierDescription
Single click
noneSelect peak
⌘Select additional peak
⇧Select peak series
⌥Zoom in
⌥⇧Zoom out
Double click
noneSelect scan
⌥⇧Zoom view to fit all data
⌘Add baseline point
Drag within chromatogram
nonePan
⌥Zoom in
⌘Select baseline points
Drag outside chromatogram
nonePan (horizontal direction only)
⌥Zoom in (horizontal direction only)
In Comparison Window
idem + ⌘Apply action to current chromatogram only
